# AI Tool Adoption Trend Analysis

## 1. Problem Statement

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming industries worldwide. Organizations are increasingly adopting AI tools to improve productivity, reduce costs, and enhance decision-making. This project analyzes AI adoption trends across industries and company sizes and evaluates the impact of AI on organizational performance.

## 2. Dataset Description

* Dataset: Global AI Adoption and Workforce Impact Dataset
* Source: Kaggle
* Records Used: 10,000
* Features: 43 columns
* Key Attributes:

  * Industry
  * Company Size
  * AI Adoption Rate
  * AI Tools Used
  * Employee Satisfaction
  * Productivity Change
  * Revenue Growth
  * Cost Reduction
  * AI Investment

## 3. Trend Analysis

The following trends were analyzed:

* Industry vs AI Adoption
* Company Size vs AI Adoption
* AI Tool vs Employee Satisfaction
* Adoption Year vs Growth
* Employee Count vs AI Usage

### Key Findings

* Technology and Finance industries show high AI adoption.
* Larger organizations tend to adopt AI more rapidly.
* Organizations using multiple AI tools show higher satisfaction scores.

## 4. Statistical Findings

### Correlation Analysis

Relationships analyzed:

* AI Adoption vs Productivity
* Tool Usage vs Satisfaction
* Employee Count vs Adoption
* AI Investment vs Cost Reduction

### Hypothesis Testing (ANOVA)

* H0: Company size has no impact on AI adoption.
* H1: Company size significantly impacts AI adoption.

Result:

* F-Statistic = 3746.05
* P-Value = 0.0

Conclusion:
The null hypothesis was rejected, indicating that company size significantly impacts AI adoption.

## 5. Visual Insights

Visualizations created:

Insights:
